About Me

I'm Amina. your brand's online voice.

I'm a Nigeria-based Social Media Manager with over 2 years of experience helping small businesses and personal brands build a compelling, consistent presence online. I've worked with business coaches, fintech startups, and everything in between.

I don't just post content. I think strategically about your audience, your goals, and how every piece of content moves your brand forward. From growing a TikTok page from zero to managing full multi-platform presences, I handle the complete picture.
